REST & RECHARGE

Weekend rest is critical for recovery. Go for a 30-min outdoor walk, stretch for 10 mins, and enjoy your meals. Your muscles grow when you rest, not when you train.

☀️

FULL REST DAY

Enjoy your Sunday. Stay hydrated, eat your planned meals, and mentally prep for the week ahead. Consistency over weeks is what transforms your physique — keep going!

ESSENTIAL TIPS
For fat loss, muscle gain, and staying consistent
💧
Hydration
Drink 3–4 litres of water daily. Carry a 1L bottle and refill it 3–4 times. Dehydration kills performance and hunger cues.
😴
Sleep
7–8 hours of sleep every night. Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep — this is when your muscles actually grow.
📈
Progressive Overload
Add 2.5kg or 1 extra rep every 1–2 weeks. Without progressive overload, your muscles have no reason to grow.
⏱️
Meal Timing
Try to eat within 60 mins of waking up. Space meals every 3–4 hours. Don't skip the post-workout meal window.
🚶
Daily Steps
Aim for 8,000–10,000 steps/day. Even on rest days, a 30-min brisk walk boosts calorie burn and mood.
🍷
Alcohol
Limit alcohol — it blocks protein synthesis by up to 30%, adds empty calories, and disrupts deep sleep.
🎯
Consistency
Results come in months, not weeks. Missing 1 meal or workout won't hurt you — quitting will. Stay the course.
📸
Track Progress
Take photos every 2 weeks in same lighting + pose. Scale weight fluctuates daily — photos don't lie.
🧂
Cooking for Fat Loss
Grill, boil, or air-fry your proteins. Avoid deep frying. Use mustard/olive oil in small amounts — fats are calorie-dense.
